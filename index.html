Three Business Engines - Revenue Split & Strategy

Revenue is built on three coordinated engines working in synergy. Each engine is designed to support and amplify the others, creating a self-reinforcing growth system.

Engine 1

L&D Capability Academies

55-60% Revenue

Description: Reusable, scalable learning academies across specialized life science data functions, delivered through dedicated retainer relationships.

Academy Areas:

  • Bioinformatics & Clinical Data Analysis (SAS, R)
  • Life Science Data Engineering
  • Discovery Informatics
  • Commercial Analytics
  • Delivery Manager Leadership Programs

Pricing Model: 45-90 lakh/year for mid-market | 1.5-3 crore/year for enterprise

Year 4 Targets:

  • 35-40 active academy engagements
  • 15-20 global retainers as main ARR base
  • 45-50 Crore revenue from L&D
Engine 2

Talent Solutions

25-30% Revenue

Description: Specialized talent hiring and placement services for life science data roles, supporting client growth and skill verification.

Talent Services:

  • Direct hiring for target accounts
  • Bench hiring models
  • Skills verification and assessments
  • Team augmentation services
  • Recurring hiring cycles

Growth Mechanism: Opens new logos, expands relationships through trust, cross-sells into L&D and Innovation.

Year 4 Targets:

  • 10-15 recurring hiring relationships
  • 20-25 Crore revenue from Talent
Engine 3

Innovation & Transformation

10-15% Revenue

Description: High-margin strategic advisory and transformation programs positioning Bversity as a strategic partner, not a vendor.

Program Types:

  • Innovation challenges & capability workshops
  • Capability transformation roadmaps
  • AI adoption frameworks
  • Delivery excellence academies
  • Capability benchmarking services

Pricing Model: 35-90 lakh per project | 1.5-2.5 crore for year-long programs

Year 4 Targets:

  • 12-15 Crore revenue from Innovation
  • High strategic value and retention
