Revenue Architecture

Year-wise revenue targets following a compounding growth curve

Year 1
6-8 Crore

Foundations, infrastructure, 8-10 anchor clients and first set of L&D academies validated.

Focus on credibility, reference accounts and delivery playbooks.

Year 2
15-18 Crore

Multi academy retainers, stronger ABM and more structured revenue motions.

India and early APAC plays with repeatable offers.

Year 3
35-40 Crore

Global account entry, leadership programs and productised academies.

10-15 parallel engagements running at any time.

Year 4
80-85 Crore

Category dominance in India, APAC and US mid market.

35-40 clients with high renewal rates.

Three Business Engines

Revenue is built on three coordinated engines

Engine 1: L&D Capability Academies

55-60% of Revenue

  • Bioinformatics, Clinical Data & SAS
  • Life Science Data Engineering
  • Discovery Informatics
  • Commercial Analytics
  • Delivery Manager Leadership

Year 4 Target: 45-50 Crore L&D Revenue

Engine 2: Talent Solutions

25-30% of Revenue

Hiring engagement and specialized talent placement for life science data roles.

  • Direct hiring for target accounts
  • Bench hiring models
  • Skills verification
  • Team augmentation

Year 4 Target: 20-25 Crore

Engine 3: Innovation & Transformation

10-15% of Revenue

Strategic advisory and transformation programs for life science organizations.

  • Innovation challenges
  • Capability transformation roadmaps
  • AI adoption workshops
  • Delivery excellence programs

Year 4 Target: 12-15 Crore
